DIGITIZATION

What is Digitization?

Digitizaiton is the process of tranlating phsyical text, pictures, or artifacts (or anything!) into digital form.

The Main Challenges:

  • Maintaining the authenticity of the original item

  • Organizing the data and gathering metadata

  • Automating the process & handling a large amount of data

How to Approach Digitization:

  • Plan and prioritize what to digitize

  • Use consistent equipment settings and file formats

  • Add clear filenames and metadata

  • Store and back up files safely

  • Review and update your system over time
